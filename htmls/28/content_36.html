Description
This skin is inspired by a plague doctor, with the intention of being darker in color but with enough contrast to neon colors such as green which is associated with infection, also purple which is associated with dreams. The idea is to be a little creepy like a bad dream or nightmare in this case. I enjoyed the process and this is my first skin I ever made. I hope you like it. Cheers.
