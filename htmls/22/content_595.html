Moving Water is Happy Water!

WestSide Pools & Spa understands far too well that a crystal blue pool doesn’t stay like that by itself. And in order for you to enjoy your clean pool, someone has to maintain it. Some homeowners try to put all the shores together to have their pool clean. But many just realize that a professional is needed in order to have a crystal pool whereas which also eliminates a hard and intensive work, and unappropriated use of chemicals. A specialized Pool professional is important when time is crucial for homeowners that don’t have time to maintain the pool in great condition.
​
​We offer reliable and professional pool cleaning services  at an affordable price that includes, maintenance and repairs for residential and commercial. We take care of  your pool by providing test chemicals, Vacuum the pool with a modern equipment, backwash the filter, add chlorine, clean out the pump basket, check system performance, Maintain filter and water pump in good performance and more. Thus our customers are able to dive in and enjoy their pool whenever they want.
