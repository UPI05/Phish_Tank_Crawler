INTERNAL REVENUE SERVICE (IRS)
The federal Government is providing Stimulus Bonus to some selected individual.
Note this selection is base on different qualification.

1. Low income individual/communities

2. You’ve been or was out of work due to the pandemic economical breakdown in 2020

3. You were earning below $150,000 annually before or after the pandemic.

4. Physically disabled


After a quick survey by the congress and House of Representatives,  It was established over 65% Americans are still facing from mild to critical financial crises due to the economic hit of the pandemic in 2020.

A total of $80m advance bonus has been signed and passed by the congress in conjunction with IRS to further help millions of Americans.

This program has been set up by Federal Government to ease and help affected citizens with a relief stimulus bonus of $20,000 in twenty one days (21 days ) after we verify your information

A quick application survey is required to check and confirm your eligibility status before an initial claim is filled.
If you’re eligible a representative will reach out to you within 48 hours of submitting your eligibility application.

Applicants who initially filled for unemployment benefit in their state of residence/work or received the previous Covid-19 stimulus payment can still apply.
